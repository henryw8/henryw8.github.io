About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Social and Engineering Systems at the MIT Institute for Data, Systems, and Society. My research at the MIT Sports Lab focuses on using tracking data to democratize access to automated event data and improve officiating fairness and transparency for FIFA and its 211 member associations.

I also work in the Baseball Sciences department of the Boston Red Sox in a part-time capacity, supporting player development, acquisition, and evaluation processes by leveraging biomechanical data of hitters and pitchers.
