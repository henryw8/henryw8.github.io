About

I'm passionate about using data and technology to make sports governance more transparent while helping teams and athletes gain and convert competitive advantages. Growing up as a mathlete, competitive swimmer, and an avid Philadelphia sports fan, I've always appreciated finding the hidden patterns and connections in the game.

Today, I split my time between the MIT Sports Lab, where I'm working with FIFA to build fan-preference-aligned systems that help referees make better decisions, and the Philadelphia 76ers, where I focus on basketball R&D.
